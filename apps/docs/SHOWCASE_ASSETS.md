# Showcase image sources

The documentation showcase uses eight images generated with the built-in Imagegen tool. The generated PNG sources remain outside the repository under:

`/home/jodie/.codex/generated_images/019f5d5e-a188-7580-8847-dfd2ea4029ad/`

Only stripped, responsive WebP derivatives are stored in `src/assets/showcase/`. Hero derivatives are 1600×1000 and 800×500; category derivatives are 960×720 and 480×360. `npm run check:assets` verifies the complete set, dimensions, budgets, WebP structure, absence of ICC/EXIF/XMP chunks, and the public SVG favicon.

## Final prompts and selected sources

Every prompt shared these constraints: photorealistic editorial industrial photography, hard contrast, low saturation outside signal colors, and no people, bodies, silhouettes, skin, explicit acts, sexual objects, text, logos, watermarks, interfaces, or screens.

### Hero

- Source: `exec-35c0b45b-4e48-45c3-bead-77b640d7ef8b.png`
- Prompt: Monumental architectural passage formed by heavy black rubber curtains, a dark welded steel grid, restrained chains and industrial hardware, with deep red signal light beyond the opening. Empty Berlin-inspired underground industrial interior with concrete walls and floor; landscape 8:5, low wide camera, strong depth and monumental scale; matte and lightly reflective rubber, brushed and oxidized steel, raw concrete, bolts and fittings.
